== Description ==


An easy way to place landing frog landing pages on your Wordpress site.

[Get a free account at landingfrog.com to create landing pages to use with this plugin.](https://www.landingfrog.com/)

###How to put a Landing Frog landing page on your Wordpress site
1. Create a free account on landingfrog.com
2. Create a landing page on landingfrog.com
3. Get your landing page id number on landingfrog.com.  It is the number at the end of the display link.
4. Install the Landing Frog Wordpress plugin from the Wordpress plugin repository.
5. Create a new Landing Frog post on your Wordpress site.
6. Place your landing page id number from landingfrog.com in the text box of your new Landing Frog post.
7. Publish the page and view your Landing Frog landing page on your Wordpress site.

###What you get when you use the Landing Pages For Landing Frog plugin

*  Place any landing page created with Landing Frog on your Wordpress site.


###Add landing pages for landing frog to any post or page

Whether you are writing a post about your company or another company, you can include a well formatted landing pages for landing frog.

###Quick Start Guide

* Coming Soon - [Landing Pages for Landing Frog quick start guide at landingfrog.com](https://www.landingfrog.com/)

###Landing Pages For Landing Frog 

Add as many landing pages from Landing Frog to your site as you like.  The Landing Pages For Landing Frog plugin lets you add to your Wordpress site landing pages and save them for when ever you are ready to use them.



== Installation ==


1. Upload `landing-frog` to the `/wp-content/plugins/landing-frog/` directory
1. Activate the landing pages for Landing Frog through the 'Landing Frog' menu in WordPress
1. Click **Landing Frog** on the admin menu to enable and set your options.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to know how to program or design? =

No.  Landing Frog uses a drag and drop editor to create landing pages.

= Is there a limit to the number of landing pages for Landing Frog I can create? =
No, unlimited landing pages for Landing Frog can be created.

= Can the landing pages be put on my homepage? =
Yes, you can put landing pages on your homepage by using the Landing Frog shortcode on you homepage.

= Can I put landing pages on any post or page? =
Yes, use the landing frog shortcode, [landing_frog id="%postId%"] on your pages and posts.
